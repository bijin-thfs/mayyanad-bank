Mayyanad Regional Co-operative Bank (MRCB) mobile banking app — a bilingual (English + Malayalam) web app targeting elderly and Malayalam-speaking users.

**Why:** The bank needs a simple, accessible digital banking interface for its local customer base who primarily speak Malayalam.

**How to apply:** All UI work must be bilingual. Prioritize simplicity and accessibility over feature richness. The target audience is elderly users unfamiliar with complex digital interfaces.
